Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Custard Powder
  • Vanilla essence
  • Dry fruits (Almonds, raisin)
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Boil the milk on high temperature and then stir it properly on low temperature until it becomes 1/4th from its original amount.
  2. Take the custard powder and mix it in one bowl of cold milk, Remember to not leave any lump
  3. Start to mix that custard paste into the milk on stove and stir it well.
  4. Mix sugar according to the taste and stir it well
  5. Finally turn off the stove and add CRUSHED dry fruits.
  6. Let the Ice-cream paste cool down and put it into the freezer for atleast 5 hours.
  7. ENJOY your Ice-cream
